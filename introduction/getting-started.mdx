## Generate Your First Image
<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Image Tool">
    Open **Image** from the main menu.
    
    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/getting-started/open-image-tool.png" alt="Opening the Image tool from Krea's main menu" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Write Your Prompt">
    Type a simple prompt (e.g., "A cat sitting on a chair").

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/getting-started/write-prompt.png" alt="Writing a prompt in the Image tool" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Choose a Model">
    Choose a model (**Krea 1** is a solid starter).

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/getting-started/choose-model.png" alt="Selecting Krea 1 model from the dropdown" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Customize Settings (Optional)">
    - Apply a style
    - Add an image prompt (start frame) 
    - Set format and resolution
    - Enable **Raw** for less filtered outputs

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/getting-started/customize-settings.png" alt="Customizing generation settings" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Generate">
    Click **Generate** and save your image.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/getting-started/generate-button.png" alt="Clicking the Generate button" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
**Screenshot placeholder:** First image generated from a simple prompt
</Note>

## Generate Your First Video

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Video Tool">
    Open **Video** from the main menu.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Write Motion Prompt">
    Type a simple motion prompt (e.g., "A bird flying over a forest").
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Choose Model">
    Choose a model (model options affect features like end frame, duration, and resolution).
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Generate Video">
    Click **Generate**, preview, and save.
  </Step>
</Steps>

**GIF placeholder:** Short clip generated from a simple prompt
</Note>

## Check How Much Compute You Have Remaining

Every generation uses credits (compute), which vary by model and resolution.

### Quick Check

<Steps>
  <Step title="Profile Menu">
    Click your profile icon (top-right).
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="View Credits">
    The menu shows "% daily credits used" with a progress bar.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Detailed View

<Steps>
  <Step title="Usage Statistics">
    Open **Usage Statistics** or **Subscription Plans** in the sidebar.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Review Usage">
    See your current plan, daily/monthly usage, and options to upgrade or buy more compute.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
**Screenshot placeholders:** Account menu with daily credits bar and Dashboard with usage stats
</Note>

## First-Time Checklist

Before you dive deeper into Krea, here are a few quick things every new user should know:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Find Your Results">
    All generated images and videos are saved under **Assets** in the top menu. You can download, enhance, or re-edit them anytime.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Credits Running Out?">
    If you hit your limit, credits refresh automatically with your plan. You can also buy a compute pack or upgrade to a higher plan.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Get Help Fast">
    Use the [Krea Blog](https://krea.ai/blog) or join the Krea community to ask questions and share tips.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Try This Now">
    Type: **"A futuristic city at sunset in watercolor"** into the Image tool to see an example result right away.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
