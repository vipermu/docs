The Edit Tool lets you change existing images instead of generating from scratch. You can fix details, expand the canvas, or make stylistic changes while keeping most of your original image intact.

## How Editing Works (Step by Step)

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Edit Tool">
    Open Edit from the left sidebar or by choosing **Send to Edit** on any image.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Mask the Area">
    Mask the area you want to modify. Use the brush size slider to be precise.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enter Prompt">
    Enter a prompt describing the replacement or modification.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Adjust Strength (Optional)">
    Adjust strength to control how much the edit changes the original.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Generate">
    Click Generate to see results.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select and Save">
    Select your favorite output, then download, save to Assets, or send to another tool.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Editing Modes Explained

### Inpainting

<Accordion title="Inpainting - Replace Specific Areas">
  **How it works:** Mask a specific region and describe what should replace it.
  
  **Best for:**
  - Changing specific objects or details
  - Fixing imperfections or unwanted elements
  - Swapping clothing, accessories, or props
  - Modifying facial features or expressions
  
  **Tips:**
  - Use precise masking for better results
  - Be specific in your prompts
  - Adjust strength to control how much change occurs
</Accordion>

### Outpainting

<Accordion title="Outpainting - Expand Your Canvas">
  **How it works:** Extend your image beyond its current borders by generating new content.
  
  **Best for:**
  - Creating wider landscapes or scenes
  - Adding more context to cropped images
  - Expanding portraits to include more background
  - Converting square images to different aspect ratios
  
  **Tips:**
  - Describe what should appear in the new areas
  - Consider the existing composition when expanding
  - Use consistent lighting and style prompts
</Accordion>

### Prompt Editing

<Accordion title="Prompt Editing - Global Modifications">
  **How it works:** Apply changes across the entire image or large areas using text prompts.
  
  **Best for:**
  - Changing overall style or mood
  - Adjusting lighting conditions
  - Modifying color schemes
  - Adding atmospheric effects
  
  **Tips:**
  - Use broader, descriptive prompts
  - Experiment with strength settings
  - Consider the original image's characteristics
</Accordion>

### Layered Editing

    Use "Send to Edit" on your result for additional changes

  <Step title="Refine Details">
    Make smaller, more precise adjustments
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Final Polish">
    Send to Enhancer for upscaling or quality improvements
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Masking Best Practices

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Precise Masking">
    - **Use appropriate brush sizes** for different areas
    - **Follow object boundaries** closely for clean results
    - **Avoid over-masking** - only select what needs to change
    - **Use feathered edges** for natural blending
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Strategic Masking">
    - **Include context areas** when replacing objects
    - **Mask shadows and reflections** when removing objects
    - **Consider lighting consistency** across masked regions
    - **Test different mask sizes** for optimal results
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Prompt Optimization

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Specific Prompts">
    **Good:** "replace with red leather jacket"
    
    **Better:** "red leather jacket with silver zippers, worn casually"
    
    **Best:** "red leather jacket with silver zippers, worn casually, matching the existing lighting and pose"
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Style Consistency">
    - **Match the original image style** in your prompts
    - **Maintain lighting consistency** with descriptive terms
    - **Consider the image's aesthetic** when adding new elements
    - **Use style modifiers** like "in the same art style" or "matching the original aesthetic"
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Troubleshooting Common Issues

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Inconsistent Lighting">
    **Problem:** New elements don't match the original lighting
    
    **Solutions:**
    - Include lighting descriptions in prompts
    - Use "matching the existing lighting" in your prompt
    - Try different models that handle lighting better
    - Consider the light source direction and intensity
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Poor Blending">
    **Problem:** Edited areas look obviously artificial
    
    **Solutions:**
    - Adjust mask edges for smoother transitions
    - Lower the strength setting for subtler changes
    - Include more context in your masking
    - Try different models for better blending capabilities
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Unwanted Changes">
    **Problem:** The edit changed more than intended
    
    **Solutions:**
    - Use more precise masking
    - Lower the strength setting
    - Be more specific in your prompts
    - Try a different model with better control
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
