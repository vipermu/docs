The Gallery is where you can share your creations and explore what others are making in the Krea community.

## Browse the Gallery

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Gallery">
    Open Gallery from the top-right menu.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Explore Content">
    Scroll to explore what others are creating.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="View Details">
    Click any item to open it in detail view.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Interact">
    From here you can:
    - **Like** or **Save** it for reference
    - **Copy the prompt and styles** to reuse in your own generations
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Publish to the Gallery

### From a Result Card

<Steps>
  <Step title="Generate Content">
    Generate an image or video using any Krea tool.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Hover and Publish">
    Hover over the result and click **Publish**.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Instant Sharing">
    The asset is immediately published to the Gallery.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/tools/result-card-publish-action.png" alt="Result card with Publish action" />
</Frame>

<Note>
**Screenshot placeholder:** Result card with Publish action
</Note>

### From Assets

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Assets">
    Open Assets and select the item you want to share.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Click Publish">
    Click **Publish** in the asset actions.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Instant Publishing">
    The asset is immediately published to the Gallery.
  </Step>
</Steps>
