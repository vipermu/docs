Everything you generate in Krea is saved automatically to your Assets so you can find, reuse, and export it later.

## Where to Find Assets

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Assets">
    Open Assets from the left sidebar or the top bar.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Browse Your Content">
    Your latest images and videos appear first in a grid.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What You Can Do in Assets

Select any item to see its actions:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="View & Organize">
    - **Open** – view the asset full size
    - **Like / Dislike / Favorite** – organize and keep track of what works
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Enhance & Modify">
    - **Upscale** – boost resolution (images up to 22K, videos up to 8K)
    - **Vary** – generate variations from the same prompt/settings
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Export & Share">
    - **Download** – save to your device
    - **Publish** – post to the Gallery
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Reuse & Reference">
    - **Reuse parameters** – start a new generation with the exact same settings
    - **Share parameters** – copy prompt + settings to share
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Management">
    - **Delete** – remove from your library
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Quick Steps (First-Time Users)

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Assets">
    Navigate to your Assets library.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select Content">
    Click a thumbnail to open it.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Choose Action">
    Choose **Download** to save, or **Upscale / Vary** to continue working.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Share When Ready">
    Use **Publish** when you're ready to share to the Gallery.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Asset Actions Explained

### Enhancement Actions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Upscale">
    **Improve Resolution and Quality:**
    - Images can be upscaled to 22K resolution
    - Videos can be enhanced up to 8K resolution
    - Uses advanced AI to add detail and clarity
    - Preserves original style and characteristics
    
    **Best for:** Final outputs, print materials, high-quality displays
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Vary">
    **Generate Variations:**
    - Creates new versions using the same prompt and settings
    - Explores different interpretations of your concept
    - Maintains consistency while adding variety
    - Perfect for finding the ideal result
    
    **Best for:** Exploring options, A/B testing, finding the perfect version
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Missing Assets">
    **Problem:** Can't find assets you created
    
    **Solutions:**
    - Check if you're looking in the right date range
    - Use search function to find specific content
    - Verify you're logged into the correct account
    - Check if assets were accidentally deleted
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Slow Loading">
    **Problem:** Assets library loads slowly
    
    **Solutions:**
    - Clear browser cache and cookies
    - Check your internet connection
    - Try accessing during off-peak hours
    - Consider reducing the number of assets displayed per page
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Download Issues">
    **Problem:** Cannot download assets
    
    **Solutions:**
    - Check browser download permissions
    - Ensure sufficient storage space on your device
    - Try downloading individual assets instead of bulk
    - Clear browser cache and try again
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
